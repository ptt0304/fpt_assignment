To connect to your Cassandra cluster:

1. Run a CQL shell inside one of the pods:

   kubectl exec -it statefulset/{{ include "cassandra.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -- cqlsh

2. Or forward the CQL port locally:

   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "cassandra.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} 9042:9042

   Then connect with your Cassandra client at: 127.0.0.1:9042

---

Check the cluster status:

   kubectl exec -it statefulset/{{ include "cassandra.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -- nodetool status
